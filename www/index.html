This is the first recipe. Follow these steps:

  1. Put the water to the kettle
  2. Turn the kettle on
  3. Let the water in the kettle boil
  4. Put the teabag and a lemon slice in the cup
  5. Put the boiling water from the kettle to the cup
  6. Wait for 3 mins to get the tea be ready
  7. Drink tea and enjoi!
